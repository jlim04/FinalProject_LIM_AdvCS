Final/Significance

What should you do with the list of colleges we provided? Keep in mind that these schools are merely ones that fit a few basic preferences of yours. In order to create a solid college list, one must consider the ratio of safety, target, and reach schools. This ratio must be close to 1:1:1. Furthermore, when deciding whether or not to include a school in your list, we highly suggest that you research each school individually. Please consider the following questions: How robust is their program for the major you are most excited about? How is the school social culture? What are the alumni connections and job opportunities? We encourage that you ask yourself further questions as such.
